Shawntil B.

We had a lovely time in the Pasture! We came to Canyon Lake on a maiden voyage with our brand new 43.5 ft 5th wheel RV, but the RV Park we had booked on the lake had no lake access and trailers packed in like sardines with no space to even set up a sun shade in the middle of Texas summer! I hopped on HipCamp, found Dot's place 15 min from the lake and booked it. She messaged me within minutes with good directions. The pasture was peaceful and spacious. We were able to spread out, lounge around, walk through the woods, ride bikes, and even play ball in the field. Dot really has a great setup and is so accommodating. There was one small issue when the water well tank ran low, but I just called her up and she had it resolved in no time. My family and I really enjoyed our stay and would definitely recommend as a place to get away from the overcrowding of Canyon Lake camping options.

Priscilla G.

We stayed over Memorial Day weekend and love this place! 15 minute drive to Canyon Lake, grocery store, public boat launch. Electric, water and sewage hookups were great. We loved having dinner with the cows, the goat, and the hummingbirds. Great place to get away and enjoy nature. Dot was such a wonderful host. She was so attentive, welcoming, and very communicative with us. We would definitely stay here again.
